# Code of Conduct — NEXUS Protocol

## Our Pledge

NEXUS is a protocol for everyone. As members, contributors, and maintainers, we pledge to maintain an open, respectful, and inclusive community regardless of experience, gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, ethnicity, religion, or any other personal characteristic.

---

## Our Standards

**Behavior that builds community:**
- Inclusive and respectful language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting and giving constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the protocol and the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Sexualized or violent language or imagery
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing private information of others without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Responsibilities

Edwin Realpe as protocol guardian is responsible for:

- Clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces:

- GitHub repository (issues, PRs, code review, discussions)
- npm package comments
- Any space where someone is representing the NEXUS project publicly

---

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer directly through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. The team commits to:

- Confirming receipt of the report within 48 hours
- Investigating and taking action within 7 days
- Maintaining confidentiality of the reporter
